Information

National Parks are protected areas managed by the National Park Service. It was establised to conserve natural and cultural resources. This was done for public enjoyment, education, and entertainment.

Yellowstone was the first national park. It was established in 1916 and the main purpose was to preserve the area for future generations. Now there are a total of 63 in the United States.
