Images

  1. Best for photos with many colors and gradients
  2. Best for simple images with few colors, line art, or short animations
  3. PNGs have a transparent background
  4. Use photo editing software to scale the image to the display size
  5. CSS only shrinks the display, but the browser still loads the full-size file, making pages slower.
