What is Paksu?

Paksu started as a concept by siblings Inbeom and Jiae Park for a food truck competition. “Paksu” means “clap/applause” in Korean — a nod to the joy and satisfaction we hope every customer feels after a bite.

Our Mission

We believe that food can bring people closer together and want to share a taste of Korea with others at an affordable price. The article Food: A Bridge Between Cultures and People from Psychology Today dives deeper into this.
