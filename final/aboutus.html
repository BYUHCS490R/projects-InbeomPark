About Us

Paksu first started as a concept for a food truck competition made by siblings Inbeom and Jiae Park.

The name Paksu has a double meaning, first it translates to clap/applause in Korean hence the slogan and the first part Pak is the Korean pronounciation of the last name Park.

Our Misson

Giving people a taste of Korean Street Food at an affordable price.
